Light Column

A site specific artwork installed in a busy commercial area in Chengdu, China. Interactive in nature, it uses fluid mechanics algorithms to reflect the motion and colours around using 4 cameras.

The encourages people to congregate, take a break and breath in midst of the busyness of the traffic and noises.

  • Date: June, 2020
  • Category: Installation
  • Client:Chendu City Management
  • Team: Lawrence Daykins, George Adamapolous, Dhruv Kumar
  • Designed and executed at Jason Bruges Studio
