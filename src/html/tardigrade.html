About project

Tardigrade is a continuos cruiser narrowboat living in London canals. She was built in 1980 and is being renovated by us since 2021.

Full interior and electrical refurbishment to make it more beautiful, livable and smart. Lots of small little sensors, custom made lighting setup, everything connected !

Team: Nina Pestel and Dhruv Kumar.

A cruise from Paddington to King's Cross in London
