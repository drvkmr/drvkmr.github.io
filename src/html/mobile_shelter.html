Date

Category

Description

An ambitious concept project to explore the design of a completely mobile and independent living space. The shelter integrates sustainable systems, efficient architecture, and mobility solutions to create a self-sufficient lifestyle for modern nomads. The design focuses on minimal environmental impact while maximizing functionality and comfort.
