With a background in architecture, I started fiddling with interactive technologies.

Which then became my passion, and ultimately my career. Have been doing this for almost 8 years now.

Now I live on a 'smart' boat in London canals, and focus on art-tech-sensible projects.

2023-Now. Independent Creative Technologist, London.

Making more and more effort to shift my practice and be good. This is not easy and border-line paralysing but there's no way around.

Get some sensors, lights, motors and similar assortments. Put them together with code and love. And voila, you have art. Now just wait for people to interact with it and look at you with admiring eyes (sometimes wtf looks). You are an artist now.

So much can be done with sensible use of modern technologies in our daily lives. And the 'so much' can be good or bad depending on who is in control. I like to keep it with me and people I know to make useful and invisible things. Connection to internet is not compulsory but can help sometimes.

Have been avoiding this because it's too mainstream but finally getting around to it. This website is the first big project. Learning more with each passing day.
